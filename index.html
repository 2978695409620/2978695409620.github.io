Experience

Toronto Hydro

This is my first coop as well as my first real job experience. At Toronto Hydro, I was mainly responsible for creating and maintaining excel macros using VBA to automate the process of analysing hydro metering data. Through this experience, I have learned much about the expectations and responsibilities in the workplace.

NCR Canada Ltd

At NCR, I was responsible for the performance analysis of NCR's remote deposit capture software APTRA Passport. I maintained and created jMeter scripts to identify performance bottlenecks and errors, and worked with developers to resolve these performance issues. I learned much about the JEE related technologies such as IBM Websphere, jMeter, Hibernate, Spring, Ant, Maven, and other important technologies including Oracle, MS Sql Server, and SOAP.
